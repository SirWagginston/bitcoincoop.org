We are proud to be the Canadian integration partner for bitpay.com -- the premier payment processing company for bitcoin. Bitpay enables businesses to accept bitcoins for payment and protects them from price volatility by converting bitcoins to Canadian dollars and providing an automated bank withdrawal service.

If you use our referral link to register for a merchant account with them, we'll receive a 0.5% commission from every transaction processed. We offer to split that commission 60/40 with any member of our co-op that signs up merchants using our affiliate ID.
