Coinfest 2015, Vancouver

We're proud to be hosting the third annual CoinFest, a decentralized, open source, international gathering of crypto-coin enthusiasts. The first CoinFest was held in Vancouver in 2013 and organized by co-op director Andrew Wagner. This years' Coinfest will be taking place simultaneously in 19 cities around the world.
