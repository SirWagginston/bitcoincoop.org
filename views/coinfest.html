CoinFest 2015, Vancouver

CoinFest is a decentralized, open source, 3-day celebration of cryptocurrency. The first CoinFest was held in Vancouver in 2013; it later spread to cities across Canada as a simultaneous convention. Now CoinFest is a holiday celebrated around the world, with 25 cities gearing up to incentivize adoption and educate the public.

CoinFest in Vancouver will last all 3 days, and continue to uphold the traditions of free attendance and the no-fiat rule. February 22 will once again be the conference, and February 21 will be a decentralized variety of events around downtown. Plans for February 20 are still ongoing. Please check out all of our events and sponsors!

For details on what's going on in other cities around the world, please see CoinFest.org.

Art Show @ the Fall

Location: 644 Seymour Street

Time: 12-9 PM

The Fall is Canada's first tattoo parlor to accept Bitcoin, and has already had multiple Bitcoin customers, including the founder of CoinFest. It is also Vancouver's first art gallery to accept Bitcoin, which makes it a prime location for CoinFest 2015 in Vancouver.

We're lining up some appropriate sponsors, and we will use the bitcoins to fund exciting attractions including:

  • Arts and crafts imported from Africa using Bitcoin, to help facilitate free international trade
  • Cryptocurrency-themed paintings and designs from Zoran Kutuzovic in Poland
  • A Bitcoin wine bar, featuring wines produced by Rollingdale, the Bitcoin winery
  • Other works of art (and tattoos!) available for Bitcoin from the Fall

Game Room @ India Gate

Location: 616 Robson Street

Time: 4-10 PM

India Gate Restaurant and Bar--Vancouver's first restaurant and the first Indian restaurant in the world to accept bitcoins--will once again be hosting the CoinFest Game Room. We're lining up some appropriate sponsors, and we will use the bitcoins to fund several giveaways and exciting attractions for you all!

This year, we will be using a chip system. All guests will be given some free chips redeemable for BTC, given with the request that you play one of the games. We have planned activities including:

  • Bitcoin poker, organized by Jeff Tay
  • Bitcoin blackjack
  • Bitcoin roulette

Please note that you are not permitted to bet with additional funds or assets. These are freerolls only.

Networking Time @ Francesco's

Location: 860 Burrard Street

Time: 5:30-8 PM

While CoinFest is intended to be fun and accessible to people of all means, we don't want to alienate those looking for a serious networking event. Thankfully, a high-class Italian restaurant in downtown Vancouver accepts Bitcoin; those looking for a more exclusive event can come to Francesco's, where the Bitcoin elite will dine on some of the finest dishes available for BTC.

Guests will have half an hour to mingle before dinner will be served at 6:00. Vanbex Group will be sponsoring the event, which will feature a special Bitcoin menu. They provide consultation and marketing services to major players in decentralization like Storj, Counterparty, Tether and Factom. A portion of funds will be used to treat contest winners, so young entrepreneurs get the chance to network with important decision-makers.
