Prepared Meals

How it Works

Our resident chef Chelsea Greenwood is hard at work every week preparing delicious and healthy meals for our members.

Deliveries are on Tuesdays and Fridays between 9am and 12pm. If you require delivery outside of these times, please contact us to arrange.

It is your responsibility to refrigerate prepared meals upon delivery or provide a cooler with ice packs in it at the delivery spot for your prepared meals to be deposited into.
