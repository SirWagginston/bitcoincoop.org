About Us

The Bitcoin Co-op a a group of people working together to promote bitcoin in Canada. We support our members by offering discounted rates on exchange and we support merchants that accept bitcoins by consulting with them on payment processing services and promoting them through our network. We also put on regular events, meetups, hackathons and info sessions for the local bitcoin community in Vancouver.

We are proud to be the Canadian integration partner for bitpay.com, the premier payment processing company for bitcoin. They enable businesses to accept bitcoins for payment and optionally convert them into dollars with automatic deposit into Canadian bank accounts.

When merchants use our referral link to register for an account with them, we receive a 0.5% commission for every transaction processed. We split those commissions 60/40 with anyone that refers a merchant to us.
