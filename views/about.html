About Us

The Bitcoin Co-op is a voluntary association of individuals united by a shared interest in Bitcoin, a revolutionary digital currency and payment technology. Through our website, social media and other forms of publicity, we promote Bitcoin and the businesses who believe in it. We also organize events such as presentations, discussions, pub nights, parties, and other creative outings to educate the public and grow the economy.

If you want some bitcoins of your own or feel like trading some in for cash, we facilitate in-person, peer-to-peer exchange of bitcoins to and from Canadian dollars. If you've never bought any before, our trustworthy exchangers can walk you through the process. We'll get you your coins discretely and at a fair price.

We know Bitcoin can sometimes be difficult to understand, which is why we provide consultation and technical assistance. Members and businesses who join our network enjoy the support of experienced professionals in software and finance, as well as PR and media resources when they are in need.

We are proud to be the Canadian integration partner for bitpay.com, the premier payment processing company for bitcoin. They enable businesses to accept bitcoins for payment and automatically convert them into dollars via direct deposit into Canadian bank accounts.

BitPay charges a 1% transaction fee in return for their currency exchange services. Members of the co-operative are eligible to receive 0.3% per transaction from merchants they refer to BitPay through our affiliate URL: https://bitpay.com/merchant-signup?oid=1035_1

Membership

As of August 26, 2013, we are still in the process of incorporating as a registered co-operative association in the province of British Columbia. Once we have had our incorporation documents and bylaws approved by the province, we will open membership under the following categories:

Standard Member

  • 2% discount off our posted exchange rates
  • Take advantage of exclusive offers and deals from participating merchants
  • Participate in the co-op -- join and access our knowledgeable community, attend our meetings and vote on priorities

Trader

  • Get listed on our website and have us refer people to you
  • Access our shared telephone hotline and email address
  • Pool your capital with us by agreeing to trade at cost with other exchangers

Merchant

  • Get setup to accept Bitcoin payments and receive support from our tech wizards
  • We'll list you in our directory and many others that track bitcoin-accepting businesses
  • We'll promote you through social media and press releases to make sure you get recognized for supporting bitcoin
  • We can host events at your venue to bring people to your business who are eager to spend their bitcoins in the local economy

Investor

  • We'll use revenue and investment capital to purchase and distribute promotional materials, support merchants, offer exclusive discounts, and grow our network
  • Investment class shares in the co-operative pay dividends on revenue -- bet on our success
