Services

The Bitcoin Co-op is an open and inclusive organization with the express purpose of spreading bitcoin adoption. We do this by providing services for...

The Public

  • Informing people about bitcoin by teaching them how it works
  • Recommending software and services to use
  • Hosting workshops on mining, security, etc.
  • Selling promotional materials like t-shirts, stickers, and merchandise
  • Local Exchange -- Contact someone from our network to arrange a meeting or drop in to our store during business hours to buy or sell bitcoins up to $3000

Merchants

  • Point of Sale -- we are the Canadian integration partner for bitpay.com and will help you get setup to accept bitcoin for payment in your physical or online store
  • Directory listing -- we'll add your business to our directory, and several others on the Internet
  • Event planning -- we host regular meetups and will bring customers to your venue

Traders

  • Use our store to meet and conduct over-the-counter trades
  • Safe rental -- keep cash or bullion on our premises

Miners / Developers

  • Come hack with us or use our space to run your mining rigs

Investors

  • Profits and investment capital will be returned to our members as dividends and used to grow our network and spread bitcoin adoption
