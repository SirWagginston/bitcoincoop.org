Meeting Procedure

  • Contact one of us to let us know your availability
  • Tell us how much you want to buy or sell
  • We'll arrange a time to meet at a public place
  • We'll look up the exchange rate at the time of meeting
  • We'll send bitcoins to your address using our phone or laptop or you'll send to us
  • We'll confirm the transaction online at blockchain.info
