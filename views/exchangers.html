Exchange

We're growing a network of trustworthy and knowledgeable individuals who are able to meet you in person to buy or sell bitcoins. We guarantee a professional experience and consistent, fair price. We want to help you exchange your bitcoins quickly and easily without having to go through an online exchange.

If you're new to bitcoin, we'll be happy to spend some time answering your questions and helping you setup your digital wallet to keep your bitcoins secure.

Meeting Procedure

  • We'll arrange a time and place to meet at a secure location
  • We'll look up the exchange rate at this site at the time of meeting
  • We'll count out the cash together and do the exchange
  • We'll confirm the transaction online at blockchain.info
