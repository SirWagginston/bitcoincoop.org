Exchange

Our goal is to establish a grassroots network of trusted individuals who we've met personally and trained to provide a consistent experience and fair price. These people can meet you locally or deal with you online to help your buy or sell bitcoins quickly without having to go through an online exchange.

Order Online

Place your order using the form below or call us at +1 604-901-8399 and we'll get in touch with you within 24 hours to setup a meeting.


Rates

Our rates are posted in the top right corner of this website. We peg our rates to the order book at cavirtex.com plus 5%

Meeting Procedure

  • We'll arrange a time to meet at a public place
  • We'll look up the exchange rate at this site at the time of meeting
  • We'll count out the cash together and do the exchange
  • We'll confirm the transaction online at blockchain.info
