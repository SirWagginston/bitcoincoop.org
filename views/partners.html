CoinOS

CoinOS is an open-source and completely FREE Point of Sale system built by co-op members. It provides merchants with a simple to use system for taking bitcoin payments and managing administration.

CoinOS works in any Internet browser, so can be used with any iPhone or Android tablet, and can be branded with your logo. It is simple to use, making staff training a doddle!

Website: www.coinos.io


Quadriga CX

Quadriga CX is a Canadian Cryptocurrency exchange platform, with offices in Vancouver, BC. The exchange's goal is to provide an easy to use platform to simplify the process of buying and selling Bitcoins.

The Bitcoin Co-op recommends Quadrigs CX to those who would like to purchase their bitcoins online at a market rate or trade live with others peers.

Website: www.quadrigacx.com
